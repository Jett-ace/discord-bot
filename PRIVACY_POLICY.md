# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 7, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use this Discord bot ("the Bot").

## 1. Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information
When you use the Bot, we automatically collect:
- **Discord User ID**: Your unique Discord identifier
- **Username and Display Name**: For displaying in commands and leaderboards
- **Server/Guild ID**: The Discord server where commands are used
- **Command Usage**: Commands you execute and timestamps

### User-Generated Data
The Bot stores:
- Virtual currency balance (Mora)
- Inventory items and achievements
- Game statistics and history
- Premium subscription status
- Custom settings and preferences
- Transaction logs

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide and maintain Bot functionality
- Track virtual economy and user progress
- Display leaderboards and statistics
- Prevent fraud and abuse
- Improve Bot features and performance
- Provide customer support
- Process premium subscriptions

## 3. Data Storage and Security

- Data is stored in secure SQLite databases
- We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data
- No passwords or sensitive personal information is collected
- Data is stored on secure servers with restricted access

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your data to third parties.

We may share data only in the following circumstances:
- **Public Leaderboards**: Your username and statistics may be displayed in public leaderboards
- **Legal Requirements**: If required by law or legal process
- **Bot Operators**: Authorized administrators may access data for support and maintenance

## 5. Third-Party Services

The Bot may integrate with:
- **Discord API**: Subject to Discord's Privacy Policy
- **Payment Processors**: For premium features (subject to their privacy policies)

We are not responsible for third-party privacy practices.

## 6. Data Retention

- Active user data is retained indefinitely while you use the Bot
- Inactive accounts may be deleted after extended periods of inactivity
- You may request data deletion at any time (see Your Rights below)
- Transaction logs may be retained for audit purposes

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your stored data
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your data (some data may be retained for logs)
- **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Opt-Out**: Stop using the Bot at any time

To exercise these rights, contact the Bot owner through Discord.

## 8. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not intended for users under 13 years of age (or under 18 for gambling features). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we discover we have collected data from a child, we will delete it promptly.

## 9. Cookies and Tracking

The Bot does not use cookies or browser tracking technologies. All data collection occurs through Discord API interactions.

## 10. International Users

The Bot may be used globally. By using the Bot, you consent to the transfer and processing of your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy, regardless of your location.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting. Your continued use of the Bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## 12. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR:
- Right to data portability
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to object to processing
- Right to lodge a complaint with supervisory authorities

## 13. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under CCPA, including the right to know what personal information is collected and the right to opt-out of data sales (we do not sell data).

## 14. Contact Information

For privacy concerns, data requests, or questions about this policy, contact:
- **Discord**: Reach out to the Bot owner via Discord
- **Support Server**: Join the official support server (if available)

## 15. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach affecting your information, we will notify affected users as soon as reasonably possible.
